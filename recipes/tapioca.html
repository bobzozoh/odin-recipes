Tapioca

Tapioca with cheese, scramble eggs and green onions

Simple fast and healthy!

Ingredients

  • 30grams or Two table spoon's Tapioca(Cassava) Flours
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Slice of cheese
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 5 grams or a pinched of chopped green onions

Steps

  1. sift the tapiocas flour(optional)
  2. in a frying pan fry the tapioca Flours for 4 min
  3. reserve in plate
  4. fry the eggs (scramble or sunshine) your choice!
  5. put the frying eggs on the tapioca disk
  6. put the cheese
  7. sprinkle the green onions
  8. Done! bon Appetite!
